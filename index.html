About

I am a Research Engineer specializing in Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Computer Vision. My research focuses on the development of Large Language Models (LLMs) for medical health applications, particularly in sexual, reproductive, and mental health (SRMH). I also work on low-resource Bangla NLP, hate speech detection, and explainable AI for medical imaging and multimodal learning.
