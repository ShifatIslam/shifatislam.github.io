About Me

I am a Research Engineer specializing in Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Computer Vision, with a focus on developing interpretable, socially beneficial AI systems. My current research integrates transformer-based architectures, LLMs, and Explainable AI (XAI) to address challenges in healthcare, low-resource language modeling, and multimodal learning.
