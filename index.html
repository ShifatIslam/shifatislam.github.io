About

I am Shifat Islam, a Research Engineer with expertise in NLP, Computer Vision, and Multimodal AI. My work focuses on domain-adapted LLMs and multimodal systems for healthcare, especially in low-resource and linguistically diverse settings. I’ve conducted research in clinical NLP, mental health informatics, and bias-aware AI, with a focus on SRMH, and have published in peer-reviewed journals and international conferences.

Research Interests

NLP specially LLM, Computer Vision in Medical & Agriculture, Vision-language models, AI in healthcare: Public health risk assessment (SRMH), Multimodal AI, Generative AI
